Patient’s and Consumerism:

At RMASA, we recognize that our patients are also consumers when it comes to their healthcare needs. Health care is a critical part of our everyday life and the ability to research and understand health issues and treatment options is a major component of our decisions regarding our care. We seek to provide accurate and up-to-date information to our patients in an understandable and user-friendly format. We realize that you, as the patient, have the final say inchoosing your healthcare provider team. We thank you for considering RMASA to help in meeting your healthcare needs.
